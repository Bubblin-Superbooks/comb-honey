What To Use In The Brood Chamber When Hiving Swarms.

(1 The use of narrow strips of foundation 1 inch or less in width in the brood chamber offers some advantages. (a When the brood chamber contains only these narrow "starters" and supers of partly filled sections are transferred from the parent colony to the new swarm at the time of hiving, there being no cells below in which to store the honey, it is taken to the supers.
