Other Apparatus.

Among the other apparatus needed in commercial comb-honey production are a honey extractor, wax press, bee-escapes, and escape « 17 » boards (), queen-excluding honey boards (), feeders, tools, etc. It is not necessary to provide queen-excluding honey boards for each colony unless some special system is followed, yet a few excluders are very desirable for various special manipulations.
